# Shipping FAQ

## Standard Delivery Times
Orders within the Netherlands are delivered within 2-3 business days.
Orders within the EU are delivered within 5-7 business days.
International orders (outside EU) take 10-15 business days.

## Express Shipping
Express shipping is available for orders within the Netherlands and Belgium.
Delivery within 1 business day if ordered before 14:00.
Express shipping costs an additional 9.95 EUR.

## Tracking Your Order
Every order gets a tracking number sent to your email within 24 hours of shipping.
You can track your order at https://track.techwidget.com
If you haven't received a tracking number after 48 hours, contact support.

## Shipping Carriers
We use PostNL for Dutch deliveries, DHL for EU shipments, and DHL Express for international orders.

## Free Shipping
Free standard shipping on all orders above 50 EUR within the Netherlands.
Free standard shipping on all orders above 75 EUR within the EU.

## Delivery Address Changes
You can change your delivery address up until the moment the order is shipped.
Once shipped, you need to contact the carrier directly using your tracking number.
We cannot redirect packages that are already in transit.
