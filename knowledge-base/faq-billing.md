# Billing FAQ

## Payment Methods
We accept iDEAL, credit card (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, Bancontact, and bank transfer.
All payments are processed securely through Mollie.

## Invoices
A PDF invoice is automatically sent to your email after each purchase.
You can also download invoices from your account at techwidget.com/account/orders.
For business customers: include your VAT number during checkout for a proper B2B invoice.

## Duplicate Charges
If you see a duplicate charge, it may be a temporary authorization hold.
Authorization holds are automatically released within 3-5 business days.
If the duplicate charge persists after 5 business days, contact us with your order number and bank statement screenshot.

## VAT and Tax
All prices on our website include 21% Dutch VAT.
EU business customers with a valid VAT number can purchase VAT-free (reverse charge).
Enter your VAT number during checkout to apply the exemption.

## Payment Failed
If your payment failed, check that your card has sufficient funds and is not expired.
Try a different payment method if the issue persists.
Failed payment attempts do not result in charges to your account.

## Subscription Billing
Premium support subscriptions are billed monthly on the same day you signed up.
You can cancel anytime from your account settings — no cancellation fee.
After cancellation, your premium access continues until the end of the billing period.
