# Returns & Refunds FAQ

## Return Policy
You have 30 days from delivery to return any product for a full refund.
Products must be unused and in original packaging.
Sale items can only be returned within 14 days.

## How to Return
1. Email support@techwidget.com with your order number
2. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours
3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
4. Drop it off at any PostNL point or DHL service point
5. Once we receive and inspect the item, we process your refund

## Refund Timeline
Refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving the returned item.
The refund goes back to your original payment method.
Bank transfers may take an additional 2-3 business days to appear.

## Damaged or Defective Products
If your product arrived damaged or defective, contact us immediately with photos.
We will send a replacement at no extra cost or issue a full refund.
You don't need to return the damaged item — just send us photos as proof.

## Wrong Item Received
If you received the wrong item, contact support with your order number and a photo.
We will ship the correct item immediately and arrange pickup of the wrong one.

## Exchanges
We don't do direct exchanges. Return the original item for a refund and place a new order.
